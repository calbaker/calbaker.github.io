# Open Source Projects

This page provides links to open source projects where I am or was a key contributor.

## [~~National Renewable Energy Lab (NREL)~~ National Lab of the Rockies (NLR)](https://www.nlr.gov/)
I was the lead developer of [FASTSim](https://github.com/NatLabRockies/fastsim)
for the entire time I worked at NREL from 2019 through 2025, and I was also the
lead developer of [ALTRIOS](https://github.com/NatLabRockies/altrios) from the
start of ALTRIOS in 2022 through the time I left NREL in 2025.

## [ETH Zürich GEG Group](https://geg.ethz.ch/)
